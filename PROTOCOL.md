This document outlines the ethical and operational standards for the Anti Vision collective. All OSINT nodes operating under this banner are bound by these protocols.

## Preamble
Anti Vision is a collective dedicated to the restoration of equilibrium. [cite_start]Our motto, "Wrongdoings are corrected equally," serves as the foundation for all operational conduct[cite: 24].

## I. Doctrine of Proportionality
[cite_start]Remediation actions must be commensurate with the harm inflicted[cite: 26]. [cite_start]Efforts must address the totality of the damage, including hidden or psychological tolls[cite: 27].

## II. Accountability & Liability
- [cite_start]**Mission-Critical Liability:** The collective assumes liability for operations conducted within vetted mission protocols[cite: 28].
- [cite_start]**Individual Liability:** Operators deviate from these protocols at their own risk[cite: 29].
- [cite_start]**Integrity of Purpose:** Assets shall not be used for personal vendettas; we act to correct, not to lash out[cite: 31, 32].

## III. Rules of Engagement
- [cite_start]**Immunity of the Innocent:** Collateral damage is strictly prohibited[cite: 33].
- [cite_start]**Verification Loop:** All targets must pass a rigorous Red Team/Verification phase to prevent confirmation bias before execution[cite: 36].
- [cite_start]**Mandatory Remediation:** If a classification error harms a genuine entity, the group is bound to a mandatory Pivot, Apology, and Payback protocol[cite: 38].
