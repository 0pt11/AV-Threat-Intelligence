# AV-Threat-Intelligence

## Mission
The Anti Vision OSINT Node is an independent initiative dedicated to identifying, documenting, and escalating systemic digital threats. We focus on the intersection of mobile ad-fraud, deceptive data harvesting, and malicious infrastructure. 

Our goal is to provide actionable, verifiable intelligence to security researchers, regulatory bodies, and platform policy teams to restore safety and equilibrium to the digital ecosystem.

## Repository Structure
- **/dossiers**: Compiled intelligence reports on identified threat actors.
- **/evidence**: Structured forensic artifacts (screenshots, logs, and network data) supporting our findings.
- **/methodology**: Documentation on our analysis standards (static APK analysis, infrastructure mapping, and DNS interrogation).

## Current Research Focus
- **Mobile Ecosystem Integrity:** Identifying fraudulent app clusters on major platforms.
- **Data Harvesting & Ad-Fraud:** Mapping infrastructure used for unauthorized user profiling.
- **Phishing & Financial Deception:** Documenting deceptive schemes targeting retail investors/users.

## Disclosure & Remediation
We advocate for **Coordinated Disclosure**. We actively report our findings to:
1. **Regulatory Authorities** (e.g., BTK and relevant consumer protection agencies).
2. **Platform Security Teams** (Google Play Policy, Meta/Instagram Ad Integrity).
3. **The Cybersecurity Community** (Independent researchers and incident responders).

## Contact
For inquiries regarding intelligence findings, technical collaboration, or to report new threat vectors, please open an issue in this repository or contact the Anti Vision OSINT Node.
